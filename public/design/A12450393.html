Persona: Robert is a 30 year old office worker. He wishes he could get more vacation time in order to travel more often. Since he has a family and bills to take care of, he does not have a lot of free time. Instead, he visits museums and walks up to displays that allow him to virtually travel to other parts of the world. This way, he can educate himself of different cultures and appreciate all the diversity there is in the world, without having to spend a lot of money traveling.
