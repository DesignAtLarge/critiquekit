Persona: Johnny is an undergraduate student. He is a really active person, which causes him wear down his shoes quickly. Johnny needs a way to quickly find shoes that fit well, are nice, and are cheap. 

 

Inch: Johnny needs a way to quickly identify nice looking shoes in the world. The watch interface uses image recognition to identify pairs of shoes.

 

Foot: Johnny needs a way to quickly purchase the shoes that are identified through the inch interface. Johnny would use the watch to identify shoes in the world, then use the tablet to make purchases. 

 

Yard: Johnny needs a way to find shoes that fit him comfortably. The yard scale utilizes 3D scanning of Johnny's foot to recommend a list of shoes for him.
