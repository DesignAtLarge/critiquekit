Lead User:

Tommy Thach is a teacher at an elementary school with a low budget. The school doesn't have enough money for him to buy supplies for Halloween activities. 

Task and Domain:

This interface is for schools and teachers that are on a low budget and need donations so their students have access to more resources to learn. The task is to request donations in a quick, convenient way, so schools/teachers don't have to worry too much about a limited budget.
