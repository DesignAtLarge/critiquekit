Lead User:

Tommy Thach is an elementary school teacher at a school with a very low budget. He asked for donations from people and stores to solve this problem. 

 

Scenario:

The school that Tommy teaches at doesn't have sufficient funds for Tommy to create a fulfilling schedule for his students. For Halloween activities, Tommy's classroom budget is only $20, but the calculated total for his activities summed up to $78. He needs to find a way to obtain the necessary supplies to make sure his students have a good time in class. The app helps users find people/stores that are willing to donate school supplies to their classrooms.
