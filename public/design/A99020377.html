Lead user: Rob is a online shopper on a budget. He utilizes multiple deal watching and online stores such as slick deals, camel camel camel, subreddits, and mass drop. He wants to make sure that he is getting the best deals across multiple website and that the deal is legitimate. 

 

Scenario: Rob wants to be able to select an item and set a reasonable price which he is willing to purchase the item. When a site is selling that item or similar item at a lower price compared to the price rob selected he will be alerted and directed to purchase the item. Furthermore he can set option for the app to automatically purchase the item if the deal is significantly cheaper than the price he expected.  

Rob is interested in buying a 144hz monitor. He filters through and finds a list of monitors on the market. He is able to see stats on past market deals across multiple websites and is given the option to be alerted at a price he sets, Furthermore he can set the app to automatically purchase from any of the brands if the 144hz 24' monitor reaches the price of 300 dollars.
