Persona: Jerry is a college student who just moved off campus, he has to buy his grocery and plan his meals. He wants to be efficient when he shops at the grocery store and be able to remember every ingredient he needs for recipes he plan on cooking over the week.

 

inch: Jerry can view his shopping that he created with the foot scale interface. He is also given a direction of the most efficient shopping route based on his shopping list.

 

foot: Jerry can search recipe and create his shopping list based on recipes. He can also select ingredient and use the match recipes button to see which recipes he can use. Create a shopping list by dragging and dropping items into the shopping list button. 

 

yard: Grocery store display. At idle show today's specials. When Mary approaches with shopping list on watch or phone the display will change to show matching deals, and possible recipe with items on her shopping list.
