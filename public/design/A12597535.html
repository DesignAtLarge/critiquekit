Lead User: 

Victor is a sophomore at UCSD majoring in Computer Science, who wants to know what courses to enroll in for next Spring Quarter, in addition to planning what classes to take and when so he can fulfill his Four Year Plan and graduate on time

 

Domain: Students enrolling in courses at UCSD (in this particular case, those majoring in Computer Science)

Tasks:

  • Display a students' remaining requirements to graduate
  • Determine which classes the student needs to take to meet these requirements
  • Help students plan their Four Year Plan so they can ideally graduate on time
  • List the possible CSE Electives a student can take, with the professors' ratings possible and the availability 
  • Recommend good graduate courses for students to take, with the professors's ratings and the course availability
  • Allow the student to register for courses for this upcoming quarter through WebReg, and recommend what to first/second pass based on which classes have less space
