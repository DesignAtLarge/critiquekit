Von's / La Jolla Colony Center | 7748 Regents Road
San Diego, CA 92122 | (858) 597-2222

Leucadia Pizzeria and Italian Restaurant UTC/La Jolla is located in the heart of the Golden Triangle between the University Town Center Shopping Mall and the scenic coastal downtown district of La Jolla. Our restaurant features a large patio, perfect for corporate get-togethers or family parties. We offer a full bar menu, wine and an assortment of beers on tap. We cater to La Jolla, University City, North Clairemont, Sorrento Valley, Torrey Pines, and Sorrento Mesa.

Our entire menu can be delivered to your home or office. And we can cater your private event.

For reservations, take-out, delivery or catering call us at (760) 942-2222.
