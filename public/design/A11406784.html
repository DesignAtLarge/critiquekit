Persona: Tim is a pretty tech-savvy guy who works as a waiter at a restaurant. The restaurant has some money to spend on new devices, so Tim is envisioning ways to increase responsiveness to customers to provide better service and satisfaction.

 

Inch: Tim needs to identify at a glance what customers would like help with and when, anticipating where he is needed next in the restaurant.

 

Foot: Tim needs to streamline the order process, giving customers an interactive menu that they can order off of without having to wait for a waiter.

 

Yard: Tim wants a nice interface that visually represents the tables and restaurant patrons, showing their status and other information such as reservations and the waitlist. It would also allow him to manage taking customer payments.
