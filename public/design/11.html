Lead User: Sophia is about to graduate from college and has a 3 garbage bags full of clothes and miscellaneous items she wants to donate to local charities. She needs to do it soon since she is about to move out. Sophia is not sure which charities need clothing donations or her other items. She feels more comfortable donating to local organizations so she can help her community and see what she donates actually being used.

 

Task: The task is to create a platform where Sophia can donate to a local organization of her choosing that actually needs the donation she has available. She wants to be able to see where the donation goes and speak with the organization she donates to directly.
