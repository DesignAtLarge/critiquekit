[PERSONA] Mike is a CG (Character Generator) operator at a broadcast production studio that produces fighting games tournament streams. As he is often on his feet, Mike wants alternative methods of editing the scoreboard overlays dynamically without having to be physically present at his desk.

 

Inch: If mike wants to view the stream from an in-house perspective, he can use his smartwatch to dynamically update the scoreboard remotely.

 

 

Foot: Containing a much more able interface, Mike can use a tablet to customize the text fields displayed by the overlay. Here, Mike can use a multi-touch swipe feature to add scores to the selected player.

 

 

 

Yard: Containing a preview of the actual overlay, the yard interface is utilized through voice commands. Mike can interact with an AI in a similar manner to an actual production studio, in which he would be directing another operator to update a particular field with particular commands.
