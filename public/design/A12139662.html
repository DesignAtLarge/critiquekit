Jane is an active student who is interested in honing her fitness skills for better overall health. She is familiar with an active lifestyle but is new to the gym and is scared of overworking herself early on. 

 

Inch: the inch scale will let her enter the number of reps for a specific workout and will notify her via haptic feedback when she has reached her max.

 

Food: the foot scale allows her to specify her focus for the day and gives her the appropriate feedback and quantitative data for her current workout.

 

Yard: the yard scale gives her an overview of her workout and the amount of stress she has put on her body. it suggests meals for recovery and allows her to see how she stacks up against other members of her gym.
