This prototype is for pregnant woman and their family to track the data during pregnancy, and learn about the knowledge to prepare for a baby.

Juliet and Romeo are expecting their first baby. Juliet needs a convenient way of keeping track of data, learning about what sheâ€™s doing wrong, get connected with other moms, and record her progress in pregnancy. 

 

The inch scale helps keep track of data on-the-go. It monitors how's the mom doing and prompts notifications and warnings.

The foot scale is mainly for learning purposes. The default articles suggested are selected based on the data retrieved during the day. eg. If the watch detects seafood and prompts a notification of "Stay away from seafood", the foot scale interface will suggest an article of pregnancy diet.

The yard scale is for the family to remember the how far they've come through the entire process. It is a wall of photos or a smart mirror that can overlap your current image with some image before so the new mom can see the physical change during the process.
