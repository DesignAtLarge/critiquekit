Lead User:

Reese is a tech-savvy college student in a history course who takes notes in lecture using his computer.

 

Scenario:

Reese desires a fast note-taking software that allows him to be minimally distracted during lecture, lay out his notes in a way thats easy to navigate and appealing to look at, and navigate old notes quickly and effectively. In the video, Reese details all the features that would allow him to do so given the current best technologies.
