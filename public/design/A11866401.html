Persona: Lise is a single mom in a large house rigged with smart home gadgets for temperature, lights, security, and media. She wants a streamlined interface that allows her to conserve her valuable time, aids her to organize her busy schedule, and helps her keep a low energy bill

Inch: Lise wants an interface to control and briefly overview the configurations of her home that is always on her.
Foot: Lise wants a uniformed interface that will allow her to change the settings in her house from any location.
Yard: Lise wants a morning debrief that allows her to organize her day and gives her a non-intrusive means of overseeing the smart home settings.
