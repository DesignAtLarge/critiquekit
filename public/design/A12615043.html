Vincent is a 21 year old college student who likes to eat at trendy restaurants on the weekends. He wants to be able to make a ticket to get a table for the hot new restaurant in town Menya Ultra Ramen on a Saturday night. He also wants to be able to know when his table is ready and check in at the restaurant. 

 

Inch:

Vincent has a smartwatch and he wants to make a ticket on the fly, be able to change his ticket, and be notified of when his table is ready without carrying around a bulky tablet. 

 

 

Foot: 

Vincent has a tablet at home and he wants to make his ticket on his tablet because it is easier to view details about restaurants. 

 

 

 

Yard:

When Vincent gets to the restaurant, he wants to see where he is in line and if he can get seated, as well as featured dishes. He can also estimate how long it will take for him to get a table.
